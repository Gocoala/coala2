Swim-it

Case Study
A smart app for Android mobile devices.
Target Market: Master Swimmers
Illustrator, Photoshop, XD

Swim-it is a high level performance smart app prototype that tracks arm strokes. It is geared for Master Swimmers, this target group is always looking to improve their technique. This smart app leverages advanced technology to achieve this goal. Ideally a waterproof smartwatch is an ideal wearable, but beta testing would determine the right approach.

TeleSphere

Case Study
Corporate Identity
Illustrator, PhotoShop, InDesign

This TeleSphere company is an entire brand that is designed to give full consideration to the competitors in the internet access provider market. Key competitive players are Telus and Bell Sympatico, which this logo has to be able to compete with. It also appeals to the global market and attracts the particular large business niche as it has a clean, modern, and no nonsense feel to it. Many components were implemented including this corporate id manual.
