Phresh Fridge

Case Study
A smart app for Android mobile devices.
Target Market: Busy families
Illustrator and Photoshop

Phresh Fridge is a smart fridge app that is a great tool to help manage fridge inventory. App users can choose to take a picture, scan the barcode, or manually enter groceries each week. Expired items are tracked and alerts are generated. There is a handy shopping list feature that can remember previous lists or create new ones. In addition, the fridge temperature can be monitored directly from the app.

TeleSphere

Case Study
Corporate Identity
Illustrator, PhotoShop, InDesign

This TeleSphere logo is designed to give full consideration to the competitors in the internet access provider market. Key competitive players are Telus and Bell Sympatico, which this logo has to be able to compete with. It also appeals to the global market and attracts the particular large business niche as it has a clean, modern, and no nonsense feel to it.
